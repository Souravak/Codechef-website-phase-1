What is CodeChef?

CodeChef MACE Chapter is one of the best and most active clubs of MACE - with a vision to promote, impart and enhance a coding culture in students. The club thus provides ample opportunities to access resources and materials for competitive programming.

Why CodeChef?

The goal of CodeChef MACE Chapter is to promote a healthy coding culture among the students by providing various opportunities for developing their skills through a variety of state and national level competitions, webinar sessions, weekly quizes etc. These equip them to stay up-to-date with the latest requisites, enrich their knowledge and make them realise the points to focus and improve on. We ignite the inner passion for coding in students by presenting it in a unique and nting it exciting way.
